Bio

Kimberly Charles has been creating diverse and attention-catching web presences for a variety of clients for over ten years. She has won the young developer award five times, and her services have been solicited by companies including Amazon. Ms. Charles began programming when she was six years old and her mother enrolled her in a summer camp. Thereafter, Ms. Charles honed her programming skills by attending various classes and obtaining internships at companies such as Google and EBay.

Her endeavours earned her a scholarship to Georgetown University, where she majored in Computer Science and Mathematics. She dedicates herself to encouraging computer literacy in Jamaica and started a company named KimKim, which develops websites for clients according to individual needs and preferences. In the future, Ms. Charles wishes to bring help Jamaica to a 100% computer literacy.
